Research1: In my latest work, I developed a machine learning model to predict the adsorption energies of thiolate-protected Au25 nanoclusters, which was featured on the cover of the Journal of the American Chemical Society.

What is unique about the this model is that its features are based on the geometric properties of the adsorbate-free, non-relaxed isomers. This makes prediction extremely fast.
